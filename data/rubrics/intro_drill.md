intro# IELTS Task 2: Introduction Paragraph Criteria

A high-scoring IELTS introduction (Band 7.0+) must accomplish exactly two things in 2 to 3 sentences (approx. 40-50 words):

1. **The Hook / Background Statement (Paraphrasing):** - It must introduce the topic by paraphrasing the prompt. 
   - It must NOT copy the exact words from the prompt.
   - It should be objective and present the context of the debate or issue.

2. **The Thesis Statement (Position):**
   - It must clearly state the writer's position or outline what the essay will discuss.
   - It must directly answer the prompt's specific question (e.g., "To what extent do you agree?").
   - It should NOT include detailed arguments or evidence (save those for body paragraphs).

**CRITICAL RULE:** Do not assign a numerical Band Score (0-9) to an isolated introduction paragraph. Evaluate structural quality only.
